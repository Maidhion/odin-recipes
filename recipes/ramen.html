Fancy Ramen

Use Simple Ingredients To Make Fancy Out Of Frugal

This recipe utilizes butter, eggs, garlic, shallots, red pepper, and eel sauce to turn your 99 cent ramen into a chef's delight!

Ingredients

  • Ramen
  • Eggs
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Minced Garlic
  • Green Onion
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Eel Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the ramen in boiling water or microwave until al dente
  2. Chop 1 green onion into small shallots
  3. Add 1tbsp unsalted butter and 1 pinch of minced garlic to a pan. Heat on low until melted
  4. Add ramen noodles to pan of butter and garlic and stir
  5. Move the noodles to one side of the pan, crack 1 egg on the other side
  6. Mix the egg scramble and noodles together
  7. Add in the shallots and 4 shakes of red pepper flakes
  8. Add 1tbsp of eel sauce
  9. Continue to stir until fully cooked, do not let eggs brown
  10. Eat The Ramen!
